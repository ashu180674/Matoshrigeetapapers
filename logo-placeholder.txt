LOGO INSTRUCTIONS
=================

Please add your logo PNG file to this directory (public/) with the filename: logo.png

The logo should be:
- PNG format
- Square or circular (recommended: 300x300px or larger)
- Transparent background preferred
- High resolution for best quality

Once you add logo.png to the public/ folder, it will automatically appear on the website.

If the logo file is not found, a placeholder will be shown instead.
